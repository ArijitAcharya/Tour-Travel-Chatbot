== Travel Documents ==

- Passport: A passport valid for at least six months beyond your planned return date is required for all international travel.
- Visas: Visa requirements vary by destination and nationality. It is the traveler's responsibility to obtain the necessary visas. Our visa service can assist you for a nominal fee.
- Travel Insurance: We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to cover unforeseen events such as trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost baggage.

== Health and Safety ==

- Vaccinations: Check with your doctor or a travel clinic for recommended vaccinations for your destination.
- Medical Information: Carry a list of your medications and any allergies. If you have a chronic condition, bring a letter from your doctor.
- Safety: Research your destination's safety guidelines. Be aware of your surroundings, secure your valuables, and avoid walking alone at night in unfamiliar areas.

== Packing Guide ==

- Essentials: Passport, visas, tickets, travel insurance, credit cards, and some local currency.
- Clothing: Pack layers and check the weather forecast for your destination. Include comfortable shoes.
- Toiletries: Travel-sized toiletries are recommended. Don't forget sunscreen and any personal medications.
- Electronics: Pack chargers, power banks, and a universal adapter if needed.
